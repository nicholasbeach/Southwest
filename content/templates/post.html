Forbes on Southwest

by Southwest Blog

Posted on April, 27th 2018 at 3:00 PM

A Forbes article delved into the three values that Southwest employees hold.

I wanted to share this article because it highlights something special about the culture at Southwest Airlines.

The values listed are, "A Warrior's Spirit, A Servant's Heart, and a Fun-Luving Attitude". As an Aggie, I understand how having a core set of values is essential for a successful organization. I was glad to discover that the values at Southwest encompasses those who take initiative, treat others with respect, and enjoy their work.
